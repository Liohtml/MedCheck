SPINE MRI ANALYSIS - Evaluate systematically:

1. INTERVERTEBRAL DISCS: Height, signal, herniation (protrusion/extrusion/sequestration)
2. SPINAL CANAL: Stenosis grade, cord compression
3. NEURAL FORAMINA: Foraminal stenosis
4. VERTEBRAL BODIES: Alignment, marrow signal, compression fractures
5. FACET JOINTS: Arthropathy, hypertrophy
6. LIGAMENTS: Anterior/posterior longitudinal, ligamentum flavum
7. PARAVERTEBRAL SOFT TISSUES
