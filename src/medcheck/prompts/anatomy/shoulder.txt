SHOULDER MRI ANALYSIS - Evaluate systematically:

1. ROTATOR CUFF: Supraspinatus, infraspinatus, subscapularis, teres minor
   - Tendon integrity, signal, thickness, retraction if torn
2. LABRUM: Superior (SLAP), anterior (Bankart), posterior
3. BICEPS TENDON: Long head, anchor
4. ACROMIOCLAVICULAR JOINT
5. BONE: Humeral head, glenoid, Hill-Sachs/reverse Hill-Sachs
6. EFFUSION AND BURSAE
