ANKLE/FOOT MRI ANALYSIS - Evaluate the following structures systematically:

1. ACHILLES TENDON:
   - Continuity, thickness, fusiform vs. focal thickening
   - Intrasubstance signal, partial vs. full-thickness tear, gap if torn
   - Paratenonitis, Haglund deformity, retrocalcaneal bursitis

2. PERONEAL TENDONS:
   - Peroneus longus and brevis: tendinosis, longitudinal split, subluxation/dislocation
   - Superior peroneal retinaculum integrity

3. TIBIALIS POSTERIOR TENDON:
   - Tendinosis, partial/complete tear (PTTD), thickness and signal
   - Associated flatfoot/spring ligament involvement

4. LATERAL LIGAMENTS:
   - Anterior talofibular ligament (ATFL): continuity, sprain grade
   - Calcaneofibular ligament (CFL)
   - Posterior talofibular ligament (PTFL)

5. MEDIAL LIGAMENTS:
   - Deltoid ligament complex (superficial and deep)
   - Spring (calcaneonavicular) ligament

6. TALAR DOME / CARTILAGE:
   - Osteochondral lesions (OCD): location, stability, subchondral cyst
   - Tibiotalar cartilage thickness and signal

7. BONE:
   - Bone marrow edema pattern (talus, distal tibia, calcaneus)
   - Fractures, stress fractures, sinus tarsi syndrome
   - Os trigonum, accessory ossicles

8. PLANTAR FASCIA:
   - Thickening, signal, partial tear (plantar fasciitis)

9. SOFT TISSUES:
   - Tarsal tunnel, Morton neuroma (forefoot), joint effusions
